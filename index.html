About

Meet Braxton

Braxton Bonds is a Christian life coach, mentor, and spiritual director who helps people grow in their relationship with Jesus while building the life God has called them to live.

He works with athletes, students, families, and leaders who want to experience real transformation — spiritually, mentally, and physically.

His approach is deeply spiritual and highly practical. He believes most people love God but feel stuck living out their faith in everyday life. That's where coaching, mentorship, and discipleship come in.
